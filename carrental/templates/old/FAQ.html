F.A.Q

Help Center (FAQ) for Hill Drive:

1. What is Hill Drive?
-> Hill Drive is a self-drive car rental service that provides you with all the privacy, fun, and convenience of your own car, without the hassles of owning and maintaining one. We offer self-drive car rentals for hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly requirements. We provide doorstep drop-off and pick-up service, allowing you to focus on your drive. This service is available in all the areas we serve.
2. Do I have to bring the car back to the same location?
-> Yes, you are required to return the car to the same location from where you picked it up.
3. Is fuel included in my booking?
-> No, fuel is not included in your booking.
4. What happens if I return the car late?
-> We understand that there can be last-minute changes in your plans. We will try our best to accommodate them. However, if a late return causes inconvenience to the next user, we cannot allow it. Please refer to our Fee Policy for more details on late fees and extension charges.
5. Who pays for parking, tolls, and inter-state entry taxes?
-> You are responsible for paying parking charges, tolls, and inter-state entry taxes based on your usage.
6. Do I need to pay fines for parking/traffic violations during my booking?
-> Yes, you are responsible for paying any fines incurred due to parking or traffic violations during your booking.
7. Refund
-> Refunds will be processed within 4 to 7 working days.
8. What documents do I need to take delivery of my Hill Drive car?
-> You will need to present your original Driving License and ID proof, which should match the details provided during the booking. Driving licenses printed on A4 sheets of paper, whether original or not, will not be considered valid.
9. How do I check-in for my booking?
-> Our delivery executive will deliver the car to your doorstep. Before handing over the car, they will provide you with a pre-filled checklist summarizing the car's condition. Please review the checklist, sign it, and notify the delivery executive or contact us if any information is incorrect.

10. What are the requirements when driving across a state border?
-> Each state has different procedures and regulations. In most cases, you will need to stop at the border RTO station to pay relevant tolls and taxes if you are driving a commercially registered vehicle. All Hill Drive cars are equipped with original copies of the RC Card, Insurance Note, Self-Drive License, and the All India Permit, which will help facilitate this process. In many states, state entry taxes can also be paid online.
11. What should I do if the vehicle breaks down or if I have an accident?
-> In such cases, please immediately notify us at Hill Drive by calling. We will guide you through the entire process and coordinate with roadside assistance and insurance. Unless it is unavoidable, please do not leave the accident scene until proper support has arrived. You may be required to file/sign formal documents associated with the accident.
Please note that these FAQs provide general information. For specific details or additional queries, feel free to contact our customer support.>
